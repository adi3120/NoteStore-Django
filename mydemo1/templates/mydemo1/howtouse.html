Help Page

  • Getting Started

    • Creating an account
      To create an account in NoteStore follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. on the home page click on the bottom left button named "Join Now" or Click on the top right button named "Signup".
      3. On the register page enter your first name, last name, email id and password, please enter valid details as it may result in login issues later.
      4. Click on signup button.
      5. If your details were valid and there were no issues, your account will be successfully created.
    • Logging in
      To login to NoteStore follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. on the home page click on the bottom left button named "Login" or Click on the top right button named "Login".
      3. On the login page enter your registered email address and your password.
      4. If your login credentials were valid, you'll be successfully logged in to NoteStore.

  • Using the tools

    • Create an Organisation
      To create an organisation:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Register or Login from the homepage of NoteStore.
      3. When you're in the welcome page after logging in, there will be 3 options:
        1. Create an Organisation
        2. Organisations by me
        3. Organisations I'm in
      4. Click on "Create an Organisation" button
      5. On the Create Organisation page, Enter the name/title you want to create an organisation with.
      6. Click on "Create" button.
      7. If you get "Successfully created" message, then you can click on "Organisations by me" button to manage your newly created organisation.
    • View Organisation's created by you
      To View and Manage the organisations created by you follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. On the welcome page, click on "Organisations by me" button.
      4. You will be redirected to "Organisation's created by you" page.
      5. Here you can see all the organisations created by you.
      6. On these organisations you can perform following functions:
        1. View all notes.
        2. Create new note.
        3. Add Users.
        4. View all users.
        5. Rename Organisation.
        6. Delete Organisation
    • View Organisation's that you're part of
      To view the organisations that you're a part of follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. On the welcome page, click on "Organisations I'm in" button.
      4. You will be redirected to "Organisation's you're in" page.
      5. Here you can see all the organisations you have joined,been added or created and are a part of.
      6. On these organisations you can perform following functions:
        1. View all notes.
        2. Create new note(Only if you're the creator of that note).
        3. View all users.
        4. Leave Organisation.
    • View Notes
      To view notes in an organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. You can view two types of organisations:
        1. Organisations created by you.
        2. Organisations you're part of.
        Choose the one you want.
      4. On the organisation page, choose the organisation you want, as there might be multiple organisations.
      5. Click view notes.
      6. Choose the note you want to visit and click view photos.
      7. Each Note can contain multiple photos. View them in a gallery view.
    • Create New Note
      To delete your Organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. Select "Organisation by me" option.
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations created by you" page.
      5. Choose the organisation where you want to create a new note.
      6. Click "Create New Note".
      7. You'll be redirected to Create Note Page
      8. Enter the title of the note you want to create.
      9. If you want to upload photos in that note, click choose files button and choose multiple image files and click open.
      10. Click create.
    • View all users in an organisation
      To view all users in an Organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. You can view two types of organisations:
        1. Organisations created by you.
        2. Organisations you're part of.
        Choose the one you want.
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations" page
      5. Choose the organisation where you want to view users.
      6. Click view users button.
      7. You'll be redirected to all users page.
    • Add Uploader to a Note
      To add an uploader in a note in your Organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. Select "Organisation by me" option.
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations created by you" page.
      5. Choose the organisation where you want to create a new note.
      6. Click "View notes".
      7. You'll be redirected to "All notes page".
      8. Choose the Note you want to add an uploader to, click "Add Uploader" button
      9. You'll be redirected to "Add Uploader" page.
      10. Enter the User ID of the user you want to add as an uploader to your note.
      11. Click Add.
    • Add photos to a Note
      To add an to a note in an Organisation
      1. To add photos to a Note you have to be atleast one of the following:
        1. Owner of the organisation that note is in.
        2. Uploader of the note
      2. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      3. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      4. You can view two types of organisations:
        1. Organisations created by you.
        2. Organisations you're part of.
        Choose the one you want.
      5. You'll be redirected to "Organisations" page.
      6. Choose the organisation.
      7. Click "View notes".
      8. You'll be redirected to "All notes page".
      9. Choose the Note you want to Add photos to, click "Add Photos" button
      10. You'll be redirected to "Add photos" page.
      11. Click choose files, then choose image files and then click upload.
    • Add users to Organisation
      To add users to your organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. Select "Organisations by me" options
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations created by you" page
      5. Choose the organisation where you want to add users.
      6. Click add users button.
      7. On the add users page, type the user id of the user you want to add.
        Note: the user you want to add must be registered on NoteStore.
    • Remove users from your Organisation
      To remove users from your Organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. Select "Organisations by me" options
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations created by you" page
      5. Choose the organisation where you want to remove users from.
      6. Click view users button.
      7. On the "all users" page click the "remove" button next to the user row you want to remove.
    • Rename your organisation
      To rename your Organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. Select "Organisation by me" option.
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations created by you" page.
      5. Choose the organisation which you want to rename.
      6. Click Rename organisation button.
      7. You'll be redirected to Rename Organisation page.
      8. Enter the new name/title for your organisation
      9. Click rename button once done.
    • Delete your organisation
      To delete your Organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. Select "Organisation by me" option.
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations created by you" page.
      5. Choose the organisation which you want to rename.
      6. Click Delete organisation button.
    • Rename a Note
      To rename a note in your Organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. Select "Organisation by me" option.
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations created by you" page.
      5. Choose the organisation where you want to create a new note.
      6. Click "View notes".
      7. You'll be redirected to "All notes page".
      8. Choose the Note you want to rename, click "rename note" button
      9. You'll be redirected to "Rename note" page.
      10. Enter new note title and click Rename.
    • Remove Uploader of a Note
      To add an uploader in a note in your Organisation follow these steps:
      1. You don't need to remove uploader and revoke image uploading privilage
      2. Once you add an uploader for a note, that uploader will be automatically removed in 10 minutes.
    • Leave an organisation
      To leave an Organisation follow these steps:
      1. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      2. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      3. Click "Organisation I'm in"
      4. You'll be redirected to "Organisations you're in" page
      5. Choose the organisation you want to leave
      6. Click leave organisation
    • Delete a photo from a Note
      To Delete a photo from a Note in an Organisation
      1. To Delete a photo from a note you have to be atleast one of the following:
        1. Owner of the organisation that note is in.
        2. Uploader of the note
      2. Visit the official site of NoteStore.
      3. Login with your NoteStore account credentials.
      4. You can view two types of organisations:
        1. Organisations created by you.
        2. Organisations you're part of.
        Choose the one you want.
      5. You'll be redirected to "Organisations" page.
      6. Choose the organisation.
      7. Click "View notes".
      8. You'll be redirected to "All notes page".
      9. Choose the Note you want to view then click "Visit note/View Notes Images" button.
      10. You'll be redirected to "Notes Gallery" page.
      11. Choose the image you want to delete, click delete button.
